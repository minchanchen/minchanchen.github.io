Biography

I am a Ph.D. student (2019-Now) in School of Creative Media, City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Manfred Lau. I received my B.Sc. degree in Mathematics from University of Science and Technology of China in 2019.

My research interests are Computer Graphics and Human-Computer Interaction. My Ph.D. works are related to perceptual-based 3D modeling, which aims to help novice users to do 3D modeling easily and intuitively based on their perceptions.
